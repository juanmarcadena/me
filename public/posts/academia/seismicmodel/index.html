2D Seismic lagrangian model:

For nonlinear soil-structure interactions

About this project

2D Lagrangian model that aims to capture nonlinear soil-structure interactions given a seismic excitation. The structure was modeled by means of dashpots ands springs. The equations of movement were further determined using Lagrangian mechanics. Four problems are tackled, the first three are initial conditions problems (that try to emulate the seismic record maximum inputs), while the fourth problem describes the physical behavior of the structure given a real earthquake record

A scientific paper was done about A scientific paper came out of this work. It is currently under review on the International Journal for Numerical Methods in Engineering and we are waiting for a response.
