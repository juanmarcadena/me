Nonlinear double pendulum

with Lagrangian mechanics

About this project

Simulation of a nonlinear and linear double pendulum. It is important to mention that the animation was made in Matlab. The solution of the equations that describe the movement of the double pendulum were coded from scratch making use of classic numerical methods. A lagragian physics framework was used in order to obtain the equations that describe the behaviour of the double pendulum. In the case of the nonlinear pendulum, the equations were first discretized by finite differences and then a multivariable Newton-Raphson (NR) solver was implemented each tilmestep in order to solve the nonlinear system of equations that arise from the FD scheme discretization. In the case of the linear pendulum, a NR solver was not needed because the system of equations is linear.
